The Two Core Interview Traps That Keep Good Devs Stuck

The job isn't just about knowing the answer—it's about delivering the answer with confidence and clarity. The best candidates demonstrate a deep understanding of why React works the way it does.

Trap 1: "Explain the Virtual DOM."

Mid-Level Answer: "It's a copy of the DOM that makes React fast." (Too vague.)

Senior-Level Insight: The real answer involves **diffing**, **batching updates**, and how it optimizes repaint cycles. The interviewer wants to hear about the trade-off, not just the speed. They are testing if you understand performance implications.

Trap 2: "How do you handle side effects in a functional component?"

Mid-Level Answer: "I use `useEffect`." (True, but incomplete.)

Senior-Level Insight: They want to know you understand the **dependency array** (`[]`). They are checking if you know how to avoid infinite loops, when to use `useMemo` or `useCallback`, and how to properly clean up subscriptions to prevent memory leaks. The interviewer is testing your ability to write stable, clean code.
