## Overcoming Challenges: Keys to AI-Powered Code Generation Success

While AI is a powerful tool, it requires thoughtful use. Over-reliance on automation can lead to code vulnerabilities or loss of human intuition. Developers must maintain a balance—using AI as an assistant but ensuring human insight guides the final product. Regular audits of AI-suggested code ensure that quality and security remain top priorities.
