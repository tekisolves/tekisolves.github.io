## Introduction

Recently, Microsoft experienced a significant outage that disrupted services for users and businesses worldwide. In an era where digital reliance is at an all-time high, such incidents highlight the critical importance of robust cybersecurity measures.

## The Outage: What Happened?

The Microsoft outage began unexpectedly, causing widespread disruptions across various services, including Azure, Microsoft 365, and Teams. The downtime affected millions of users, leading to productivity losses and significant frustration. Initial speculations ranged from technical glitches to potential cyberattacks, creating a wave of concern.

## Identifying the Culprit: The Role of Cybersecurity

When an outage occurs, cybersecurity teams spring into action to identify the root cause. This involves thorough diagnostics, monitoring unusual activities, and ruling out potential threats. The initial steps are crucial in determining whether the issue is due to a technical fault or a malicious attack.
