## Pop Culture in Tech: Where Innovation Meets Entertainment

Welcome to *Pop Culture in Tech*, a new series where we uncover the exciting ways pop culture and technology come together to shape the world around us. From AI-driven innovations inspired by sci-fi movies to gaming breakthroughs drawn from comic book universes, this series will explore how entertainment isn't just influencing tech—it's driving it forward.

## The Impact of Pop Culture on Technology

Pop culture has always had a profound impact on technological advancements. Films, music, and video games are inspiring next-gen innovations, influencing everything from AI systems to the virtual worlds we interact with today. We’ll be diving deep into how entertainment fuels the evolution of technology and the ways in which both worlds inspire one another.
