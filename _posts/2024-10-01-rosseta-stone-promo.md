Language acquisition is the process of gaining proficiency in a new language, a skill that extends beyond mere vocabulary. It involves understanding grammar, pronunciation, and cultural nuances. Rosetta Stone demystifies this process with its unique immersive approach, allowing learners to think and communicate in their new language naturally.
